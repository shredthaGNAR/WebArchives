Windows only for now, but it's quick and painless.

  1. Put your phone in USB debugging mode.
  2. Connect your Droid 4 to the computer via USB
  3. Make sure you have the latest drivers and what-not (get them here if you don't)
  4. Download Dan Rosenberg's exploit
  5. Extract to a folder, and double-click run.bat.

That's it. Easy as pie. And (cough) free.
