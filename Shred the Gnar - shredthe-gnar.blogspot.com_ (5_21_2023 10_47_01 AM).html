The stats i predicted transpired: 70 SOG, 8+ power plays etc however only 3 found the net. Thats how gambling works; its gambling! There is no sure thing (well lets not get in to that, point shaving people on the take etc) It is all about the long run, which parallels with a lot of things in life and business. If you continue to make decisions that are + EV (have positive expected value) in the long run you will be rewarded. Thats what i try to maximize in my life: making +EV decisions no matter the circumstance and then let the cards fall where they may. And in the interim i may experience a period of loss but things will get better because if i am making the correct decisions things will balance out due to things regressing to the mean. Thats what the long run is all about: regression to or toward the mean. If i keep doubling down on 11 10x and never win that doesn't mean i am expected to hit the next 11, but it does mean that things will balance out, you just need to have the appropriate length to experience it. That is why bankroll management is so important: it allows you to survive the downs w/o having to decrease your bet size b/c then when it does start to revert back to the average you won't reap the full benefits.
