      8. you will get a popup that the Mac cannot check the file for malware. Go to prefs / privacy again and allow it. (YouTube the error for a tutorial video)
      9. After the installation is done, open Adobe Zii. The system preferences will block it, click ok. Open system preferences, go to security and privacy, go to general and click open anyways. Now click open in the prompt given. In Adobe Zii click patch or drag. It will ask for root permission, click ok and put in your password. That’s it !
