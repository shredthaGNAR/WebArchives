About Geotiller

We offer two products, INFOMAPs and Geotiller Enterprise. INFOMAPs is a project-based mapping solution where real estate professionals can add content (articles, due diligence, photos, comps etc.) to a map and share with partners, colleagues and clients. Geotiller Enterprise is a patent-pending technology to onboard a real estate organization's network file drive to a map interface.
