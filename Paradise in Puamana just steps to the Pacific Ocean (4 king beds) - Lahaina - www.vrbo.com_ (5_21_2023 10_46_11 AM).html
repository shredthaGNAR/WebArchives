Welcome to Puamana, an oceanfront tropical oasis. The property is 30 acres of lush tropical gardens, open spaces, and beachfront. It is located in the most southern tip of Lahaina, the sunniest, warmest, and driest area on Maui. There is no wonder why early Hawaiian Royalty preferred to live in Lahaina.

Historic Lahaina Town is a 15-minute walk, a 5-minute drive or $10 Cab Ride into a Central Lahaina a lively oceanfront town with a harbor, restaurants, bars, shopping and attractions.

Welcome to Puamana 177-1! One of the largest 2-bedroom 3-bathroom, 2 story units. It boasts 1250 SQFT of living space and 220 SQFT of lanai. There are 4 king beds, 2nd floor bedrooms have kings, and the 1st floor has 2 king murphy beds that can be separated with a sliding room divider. The king murphy in the dining area requires the kitchen table on sliders to be moved into the TV room. 177-1 unique front lanai has an ocean peekaboo to view & enjoy the sounds and views of the ocean waves!

Puamana Amenities

Puamana Resort has 3 Pools to relax and catch some rays. The stunning Ocean Pool, The Dolphin Pool (kids) and the largest one the Mountain Pool. The Puamana Clubhouse oceanfront patio, adjacent to Ocean Pool it is a unique ocean front patio with pool lounger chairs, and tables with chairs. There is bar area on the patio open to all guests that has a fridge, a sink, a 50” TV with cable tv, and a bathroom if you need to take a break from the sun or want to spend a great sunset on this beautiful patio. There is an oceanfront gym located just above the bar area on the 2nd floor. Workout with an ocean front view! There is a usage fee payable to Puamana directly of $25 a day or $100 a week to use this facility. Puamana now has newly updated pickleball court adjacent to the mountain pool. All necessary equipment is provided no charge. The is also a tennis court which Puamana provides all necessary equipment no charge. Reserve at the Puamana Clubhouse.
