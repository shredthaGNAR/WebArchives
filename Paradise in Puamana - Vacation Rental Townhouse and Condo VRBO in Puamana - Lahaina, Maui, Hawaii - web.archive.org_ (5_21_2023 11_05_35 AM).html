Why Stay With Us?

Vacation in Paradise

Our townhouse vacation rental properties are located in Puamana; an exclusive 30-acre oceanfront community of low-rise townhomes and condos nestled along the sunny south shore of Lahaina; a historic town located on the west side of Maui, Hawaii. Puamana's grounds include three pools, two tennis courts, a clubhouse with a library and ample places to surf, snorkel, bike and walk around taking in the island charm and beauty. Palm trees, soaring banyan trees and lush tropical landscaping scour the ground, making Puamana an ideal paradise getaway. Visitors are treated with stunning views of Lanai and Molokai islands. While on vacation in Puamana, one cannot but enjoy sunsets on the private beach feet away from our properties or watch the whales and dolphins in the Pacific Ocean. Garden, mountain and ocean views surround visitors and the waves provide a tranquil feeling, unparalleled anywhere else in Maui. Puamana provides the perfect vacation destination for families, couples or a romantic getaway: so be sure to book your vacation soon and experience Paradise in Puamana!

Our Townhomes are Best on the Island

Our townhomes have both been remodeled and updated with the best in modern luxuries and appliances. We have combined the island of Maui charm with the amenities of today to make a perfect place to vacation. Our townhomes boast ocean-views as well as garden and mountain views. They are perfect for entertaining with outdoor bbq and dinners outside. We offer master suites and guest bedrooms. The beach is only steps away from our townhouses. They are clean and inviting and have air conditioning.

Better Than a Hotel and More Amenities

When staying in Puamana in one of our vacation townhouses you can experience Maui better. There is no need to even leave the expansive Puamana community. Our townhomes are steps from a private beach with a surf spot and 1/2 mile of oceanfront. There are three (3) pools including a beach side pool, as well as a children's pool. In addition, there is a gym and fitness center and 2 tennis courts in the Puamana community. To relax, there is the historic Planation Clubhouse with a lending library filled with books, a card room, reading parlor and reflecting pool. Not to mention all the grass fields that litter the grounds to run and play on. It is more quaint and less crowded and better.

Best Location on the Island

Shopping, great restaurants and the most exciting nightlife on Maui is only a short walk away to Lahaina via a short trail from the Puamana Community. The neighboring town of Lahaina is an historic Hawaii town filled with the best shops, restaurants and nightlife on Maui. Lahaina Town beckons you to enjoy the bustling harbor and its variety of activities from ocean sports, snorkeling, surfing, boogie boarding, jet skiing, parasailing, kayaking, whale watching, fishing or relaxing on a dinner cruise. Neighboring Kaanapali offers world famous golf courses for golfers of all skill sets to enjoy. Staying in our Puamana vacation rental properties allows you to enjoy the island of Maui by opening the front door. With so such to do in Maui, make sure to read our Puamana vacation rental Blog detailing activities and other things to do in Maui and Puamana while on vacation.
